Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 oz breadcrumbs
  • 16 oz marinara sauce
  • 4 oz shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F and lightly grease a baking sheet
  2. Slice chicken breasts in half horizontally
  3. Add egg in a small bowl and in another seperate bowl add bread crumbs. Dip the chicken into the egg and then into the breadcrumbs. Place coated chicken on the baking sheet
  4. Bake in the oven until cooked through, about 25-30 minutes
  5. Add the jar of marinara sauce into a baking dish. Place chicken breasts over the sauce and cover with any remaining sauce. Top with mozzarella and parmesan cheese
  6. Continue baking until cheese is melted, about 10 minutes
